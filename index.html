Get to know us

Who we are

We're a tight-knit group of three who make things together. No investors, no deadlines we didn't set — just people who stay up too late building stuff they care about.

Our Mission

Build things that didn't exist before.

It's easy to make something when you're genuinely obsessed with it. That's the only filter we apply — if we're excited to build it, we build it.

Our Motivation

The next idea is always the best one.

We don't run out of things to make. Every project opens three more doors. That's what keeps us going — not deadlines, not money, just the next interesting problem.

Our Projects

Across mediums, across genres.

Games, software, stories, tools — we move between them depending on where the idea fits best. If it's interesting to build, that's enough of a reason.

What makes us different

Small, but we mean it.

Our Story

Like all great things, we started small — a few people with a shared obsession and nowhere better to be. Lightspeed Studios grew from late nights and too many ideas.

Our Team

Three people. Each brings something different. Together we cover more ground than most teams twice our size, without the meetings.

Our Values

Make things you're proud of. Ship when it's ready. Don't compromise the idea for a shortcut. That's basically the whole rulebook.

About us

Behind Lightspeed Studios

We're three people who make things because we can't really imagine doing anything else. No big backstory, no corporate origin — just a shared drive to build worlds, tell stories, and ship projects that we'd actually want to experience ourselves. Everything we make starts as a blank file and a stupid idea. Most of those ideas turn into something real.
